A house painter has to paint a rectangular wall. The painter 
knows the height and width of the wall. The painter knows that 
with a liter of paint he can paint 5 m2. To calculate:


-the number of liters it needs knowing that you need to add 10% 
to be sure you can finish the job;

-The cost in € for the purchase of the paint knowing that each 
liter of paint costs € 13 / l + 22% VAT;

-The time (in hours) that the painter takes knowing that he can 
paint 15 m2 of wall per hour;

-The total cost for carrying out the entire work (paint + labor) 
knowing that the painter costs € 25 per hour.
